What i have done so far!!

Project 1: A dispenser is a device that uses RFID sensors to automatically deliver items based on tags.
It's powered by servo motors and comes with an Arduino UNO,
which acts as the microcontroller. The sensor detects each item and sends out a signal to the servo motors, which then deliver the item.
You can program the system to recognize specific tags, keep track of your inventory, and manage your dispensing process.
It's a great way to automate item dispensing in a variety of ways, like a vending machine or an inventory management system.
Project 2: The swimming pool monitoring system integrates a rain sensor, TDS sensor,
and pH sensor to ensure water quality and safety. The system continuously measures TDS levels,
pH balance, and detects rainfall, transmitting real-time data to a central monitoring unit. If any parameter exceeds
predefined thresholds, the system triggers alarms and notifications for immediate action, helping maintain a clean, safe,
and comfortable swimming environment.
